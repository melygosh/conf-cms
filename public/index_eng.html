Conference Topics

The aim of the Conference:

Discussion of new ideas in the field of strategic management, portfolio management, program and project management.

Section 1. Integration of Strategic Management with Portfolio, Program and Project Management: Models, Methods, and Techniques

  • Forecasting in strategic management, project and program management.
  • Models and methods of strategic management, portfolio, program and project management.
  • Contemporary marketing strategies.
  • Portfolio selection and optimization.
  • Balanced scorecard.
  • Business-process modeling and optimization.
  • Information technology in strategic management, portfolio, program and project management.

Section 2. Managing ІТ Projects

Speeches by experts in IT project management

Section 3. Application of Effective Management Technologies in Practice.

Speeches by experts in management. Speeches by students graduated in project management about their professional achievements.

For Authors (Bulletin of the NTU "KhPI")

General Terms

  1. Papers submitted to the Program committee consideration , must fully comply with design requirements. Otherwise, the paper will be returned to the author for refinement or the publication will be refused.
  2. In case of their full compliance with the requirements, papers will be send to a double anonymous review.
  3. If the paper received a negative review its publication may be denied. In this case, the Program committee reserves the right to return the author's paper for revision.
  4. The paper recommended by reviewers and the Program committee should be paid in accordance with section "Price" in the allotted time, which is defined in Section "Important Dates" . In case of late publication costs payment, the Program committee reserves the right not to publish the paper. The publication payment in this case will be returned to the author in full.
  5. Papers that have successfully passed the review process and were promptly paid will be published in the Bulletin of NTU "KhPI". Series: Strategic Management, Portfolio, Program and Project Management . The journal is included in the list of scientific specialised editions of Ukraine; abstracted and indexed in the 25 international scientometric databases, repositories, libraries, search engines, and catalogues.
  6. All conference papers that will be published in the journal will have DOI numerical identifiers

Paper Requirements and Templates

Papers can be written in Ukrainian, Russian or English (the language is chosen personally by the author).

The volume of the article - not less than 5 full pages, which should be designed in the Microsoft Word editor.

The paper should be typed in template and fully comply with the requirements.

Ukrainian Paper Template (in Ukrainian)
Russian Paper Template (in Russian)
References in Harvard Style (in Ukrainian)
Program Committee Paper Application template (in Ukrainian)
Act of examination (in Ukrainian)

To Program Committee should be submitted

  1. Application for participation or Registration form
  2. Program Committee Paper Application signed by all authors (scan copy)
  3. Paper e-version (to E-mail)
  4. Act of examination about the possibility of publishing materials in open print (scan copy)
  5. Copy of the payment check (the organizational fee and the publication cost payment)

For Authors (Іnternational workshop IT Project Management)

Paper Requirements and Templates

Materials of International workshop IT Project Management submitted to the Program committee consideration should be completed according to the requirements "splnproc1703".

1. The standart paper according to the latest recommendations should be 10 pages using the template.

2. The list of references may not exceed 20 items and only 20% of references can be own scientific papers.

3. The number of authors of one material may not exceed 5 people.

4. No more than two papers by one of the authors are accepted for the workshop.
